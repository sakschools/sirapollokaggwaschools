In a notable development aimed at strengthening early childhood and lower primary education, Sir Apollo Kaggwa Boarding Primary School, Old Kampala, has officially introduced a Day Section for learners from Pre-Primary to Primary Three (P3). This important expansion is designed to widen access to quality education while responding to the growing demand from parents for flexible learning options for younger children.

The newly introduced Day Section has been designed to provide a safe, structured, and nurturing learning environment where our youngest learners can build strong academic foundations while returning home each day. We believe this arrangement is especially beneficial for Pre-Primary and lower primary pupils, as it allows them to grow under the combined guidance of dedicated teachers at school and supportive care from their families at home.

![](/img/670551.jpg)

As a school, we have taken this step following consistent feedback and requests from parents who expressed the need for a reliable day schooling option for early years education. We are therefore proud to extend our services in a way that not only increases access but also maintains the high standards of teaching and learning that define the Sir Apollo Kaggwa Schools fraternity.

Parents have warmly welcomed this development, describing it as both practical and reassuring. Many have appreciated the opportunity for their children to learn in a trusted academic environment during the day while still enjoying strong family bonding and supervision in the evenings.

In the early years section, we remain committed to delivering a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, communication skills, and social development. Our teachers have carefully structured the programme to meet the developmental needs of learners in Pre-Primary, Primary One, Primary Two, and Primary Three, ensuring that every child is given the opportunity to grow in confidence and ability.

We also wish to reassure all stakeholders that our academic standards remain consistent across all sections of the school. Whether in the boarding or day programme, our focus on discipline, excellence, and holistic development remains unwavering.

Beyond classroom learning, we believe the Day Section will further strengthen parental involvement in education. With learners returning home daily, parents will have more opportunities to monitor progress, support learning activities, and actively participate in shaping their children’s academic journey.

We take immense pride in this remarkable development, which reflects our unwavering commitment to providing inclusive, accessible, and high-quality education. As we continue to grow and respond to the evolving needs of our community, we remain steadfast in our mission to nurture every learner through a strong educational foundation that inspires academic excellence, character development, and lifelong success.
